# Studio Terra Verde Website Requirements

## Project Summary

Studio Terra Verde is a Slovak landscaping and greenery studio. The website should present the company as a premium partner for luxury landscape design, high-quality implementation, and living green spaces for exterior and interior environments.

The final website should feel clean, modern, luxurious, calm, architectural, and nature-led. It should communicate craftsmanship, trust, design sensibility, and long-term value rather than looking like a generic gardening service.

## Source Materials

- `sources/website_raw.pdf`: rough website structure, draft navigation, content, section order, and image placeholder notes.
- `sources/terra-verde-client-vision.jpeg`: visual direction, homepage mood, layout, color palette, typography feel, and component style.
- `sources/terra-verde-logo-light.jpeg`: logo on light background.
- `sources/terra-verde-logo-dark.jpeg`: logo on dark forest green background.

## Brand Direction

### Brand Name

Use the brand as:

**STUDIO TERRA VERDE**

Supporting descriptor from the client sources:

**Krajinné a záhradné úpravy**

The English phrase in the raw homepage, "Your partner for luxury landscape design, premium implementation, and living green spaces", should be treated as a positioning idea. The primary site language should remain Slovak unless the client requests a multilingual site.

### Visual Personality

The website should express:

- Premium landscaping and garden architecture.
- Clean, modern, luxurious presentation.
- Calm confidence rather than loud marketing.
- High-end residential and commercial outdoor spaces.
- Strong connection between architecture, greenery, material quality, and craft.
- Natural warmth through bronze/sand accents without becoming rustic.

### Logo Usage

Use the provided Terra Verde monogram/wordmark as the brand anchor.

- On dark forest green backgrounds, use the dark-background logo treatment or a light/bronze logo variant.
- On white or warm sand backgrounds, use the light-background logo treatment.
- Preserve wide letter spacing and the refined serif character of the logo.
- Avoid compressing, recoloring into bright greens, or placing the logo on visually noisy imagery.

## Color Palette

Use the palette from `terra-verde-client-vision.jpeg` as the primary visual system:

| Token | Hex | Usage |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Deep Forest Green | `#1B3022` | Main header, footer, dark CTA bands, premium background sections |
| Warm Sand / Travertine | `#D8C3A5` | Secondary backgrounds, subtle dividers, refined neutral surfaces |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | Main page background, content sections, high-contrast space |
| Charcoal Black | `#222222` | Body text, headings on light backgrounds |
| Muted Bronze Accent | `#9A7B4F` | Buttons, borders, icons, labels, small decorative lines |

Additional guidance:

- Do not use bright saturated green as a primary UI color.
- Keep accents restrained. Bronze should highlight actions, icons, borders, and small labels, not dominate full sections.
- Dark sections should feel rich and editorial, not heavy. Use generous spacing and strong contrast.
- Use white and warm neutral areas to keep the site clean and modern.

## Typography Direction

The client vision uses a luxury editorial pairing:

- Large, elegant serif display headings with high contrast and refined proportions.
- Clean sans-serif body text and navigation.
- Uppercase labels with letter spacing for eyebrow text, menu items, buttons, and small calls to action.

Implementation guidance:

- Use a premium serif for H1/H2 headings, similar in feeling to Cormorant Garamond, Playfair Display, Libre Baskerville, or another refined editorial serif.
- Use a clean sans-serif for body text and UI, similar to Inter, Manrope, Helvetica, or a comparable modern typeface.
- Keep letter spacing moderate and reserved for uppercase labels/navigation.
- Do not use decorative script fonts or overly rustic typography.

### Veľkoformátové Kvetináče

This section is not fully specified in the source and needs client confirmation.

Known client intent:

- The client wants to sell large-format planters.
- The source mentions a webshop-style or catalog-style page with prices.
- The client references supplier assortment from Nieuwkoop Europe:
  `https://www.nieuwkoop-europe.com/en/planters?sortBy=name_asc&limit=30`
- The client wants a similar assortment style, but with visible prices.

Requirements to clarify:

- Should this be a full e-commerce webshop with cart and checkout, or a product catalog with inquiry/request buttons?
- Who owns product data entry and maintenance?
- Will prices be fixed, price-on-request, or shown with/without VAT?
- Can supplier images and product data be legally reused, or must original images/data be created?
- What categories, filters, materials, colors, sizes, and price fields are required?
- Should product availability be shown?
- Should orders be handled online or through contact form/email?

Recommended MVP:

- Product catalog page.
- Product grid with filters for material, size, color, usage, and price range.
- Product detail pages or modal previews.
- CTA per product: `Dopytovať produkt` or `Požiadať o cenovú ponuku`.
- Visible price if the client provides final pricing.
- No payment checkout until business rules are confirmed.

## Image Generation Requirements

Where the PDF contains rough placeholder notes for visuals, generate or source imagery matching the intended scene rather than displaying literal placeholder text.

General image style:

- Realistic premium architectural photography.
- Slovak/Central European residential and commercial context where possible.
- Modern landscaping, mature greenery, refined hardscape materials.
- Warm natural or golden-hour light.
- No cartoon, low-cost stock feel, exaggerated saturation, or cluttered scenes.

Recommended image ratio usage:

- Homepage hero: wide landscape, approximately 16:9 or wider.
- Service cards: square or 4:3 crops.
- Project references: mixed 4:3 and portrait crops, but maintain a controlled grid.
- Product catalog: consistent product thumbnails on clean neutral backgrounds.

## Component And Interaction Requirements

Visual style:

- Thin borders, refined spacing, premium editorial rhythm.
- Buttons with bronze fill or bronze outline.
- Minimal hover states: subtle color shift, underline, arrow movement, or border highlight.
- Icons should be thin-line botanical/service icons in bronze.
- Avoid large rounded cards, heavy shadows, loud gradients, and generic stock blocks.

Recommended components:

- Header with logo, navigation, CTA.
- Hero section.
- Service card grid.
- Split content sections with image and text.
- Dark CTA band.
- Reference/project gallery.
- Product catalog grid for planters.
- Contact form.
- Footer with navigation, contact, and logo.

Responsive behavior:

- Mobile hero should keep the brand and main CTA visible without requiring excessive scrolling.
- Navigation should collapse into a menu.
- Service cards should stack cleanly.
- Product filters should be accessible but not dominate the mobile layout.
- Text must remain readable and not overlap images.
